Mail Merge — Next Steps

Your CSV was downloaded. Each row is a fully personalised email — just follow these steps in Word:

  1. Open Microsoft Word → new blank document → click the Mailings tab → Start Mail Merge → E-mail Messages.
  2. Click Select Recipients → Use an Existing List… and choose the CSV you just downloaded.
  3. Click in the document body, then go to Insert Merge Field and insert «Body». That single field contains the complete personalised message for each student.
  4. Click Finish & Merge → Send E-mail Messages… Set To = Email, Subject line = Subject, then click OK. Word sends everything through your open Outlook in one shot.
Tip: Outlook must be open and logged into your school account before clicking OK in step 4. Word uses whatever account Outlook is signed in to.
